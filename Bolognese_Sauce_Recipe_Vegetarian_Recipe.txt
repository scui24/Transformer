Bolognese Sauce Recipe - Vegetarian Style

Transformations:
- 4 slices bacon, cut into 1/2 inch pieces -> 4 slices vegan bacon, cut into 1/2 inch pieces
- 1 pound lean ground beef -> 1 pound lean ground tempeh
- ½ pound ground pork -> ½ pound ground jackfruit
- ½ cup chicken stock -> ½ cup tofu stock

Original Recipe:
Ingredients:
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 large onion, minced
- 4 slices bacon, cut into 1/2 inch pieces
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1 pound lean ground beef
- ½ pound ground pork
- 1 (28 ounce) can Italian plum tomatoes
- ½ pound fresh mushrooms, sliced
- 6 ounces tomato sauce
- 2  carrots, shredded
- 1 stalk celery, chopped
- ½ cup dry white wine
- ½ cup chicken stock
- ½ teaspoon dried basil
- ½ teaspoon dried oregano
- salt and pepper to taste
- 1 pound pasta

Steps:
1. Warm oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
2. Sauté onion, bacon, and garlic in hot oil until bacon is browned and crisp, about 10 minutes.
3. Use a slotted spoon to transfer bacon mixture to a paper towel-lined plate to drain; set aside.
4. Place the same large skillet over medium-high heat.
5. Cook and stir ground beef and pork in the hot skillet until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes.
6. Drain and discard grease.
7. Stir bacon mixture, tomatoes, mushrooms, tomato sauce, carrots, celery, wine, stock, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper into meat mixture.
8. Bring to a boil.
9. Cover, reduce heat, and simmer sauce for 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
10. Meanwhile, bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
11. Add pasta and cook in boiling water until al dente, 8 to 10 minutes; drain.
12. Serve sauce over hot pasta.
